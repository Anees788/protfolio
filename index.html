About the College

Chevalier T. Thomas Elizabeth College for Women is a premier educational institution dedicated to empowering women through quality education. Established with a vision to foster academic excellence and holistic development, our college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs to suit the diverse aspirations of young women.

Courses Offered

We offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses across different disciplines. Some of the popular courses include:

  • BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS
  • B.SC.PHYSICS
  • B.A ENGLISH
  • B.SC.CHEMISTRY
  • B.SC.MATHEMATICS

Admissions

Admission to Chevalier T. Thomas Elizabeth College for Women is based on merit and the eligibility criteria defined by the respective courses. Students are required to submit an application form along with the necessary documents. For detailed admission instructions, please visit our Admissions page or contact our office.
